WHAT WE DO?


Hunger Eradication Programme


The RAH Africa Hunger Eradication Programme consists of various activities that involve donors, volunteers and beneficiaries.


The process begins with the securing of donations to host Meal Packaging Events, and culminates in the distribution of meals through the Results Oriented Nutrition Programme called ECD Connect.


This programme monitors and evaluates the progress of beneficiaries, ensuring maximum impact.
